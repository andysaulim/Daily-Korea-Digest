Top Stories

Seoul-Paris elevate ties to 'global strategic partnership' amid Mideast tensions

President Lee Jae-myung and French President Emmanuel Macron upgraded bilateral relations to a 'global strategic partnership' during Friday's summit, agreeing to cooperate on safe passage through the Strait of Hormuz as Middle East tensions escalate. The leaders signed agreements on energy cooperation, defense industrial collaboration, and coordinated Indo-Pacific strategies, with France becoming Korea's first European partner at this diplomatic level.

So what: Affects energy security planning ahead of the May 29 Shangri-La Dialogue

North Korea evacuates villages near Sohae as satellite site construction accelerates

North Korea evacuated multiple villages near the Sohae Satellite Launching Ground as satellite imagery confirms construction upgrades entering a new stage at the facility. NK Pro analysis of April 1 Planet Labs imagery shows significant progress on infrastructure improvements, with evacuations suggesting operational preparations ahead of the April 15 Day of the Sun holiday.

So what: Directly relevant to April 15 Kim Il Sung birthday provocation window

Dong-A

Thousands rally on first anniversary of Yoon Suk Yeol's Constitutional Court ouster

Nearly 1,000 protesters gathered at Seoul's Constitutional Court Saturday commemorating the first anniversary of former President Yoon Suk Yeol's removal from office for illegitimately declaring martial law on December 3, 2024. A district court sentenced Yoon to life in prison in February after convicting him of insurrection for deploying troops to the National Assembly.

Trump tariffs: S. Korea says simplified US metal levy system will ease burden
South Korea's trade ministry said Saturday that a simplified US tariff calculation system for steel and aluminum imports will reduce administrative burdens for Korean exporters. DPRK · NYT Korea
Opinion | Trump May Be Turning Iran Into Another North Korea
A New York Times analysis examines parallels between the Trump administration's Iran policy and the failed North Korea denuclearization efforts of 2017-2019. The piece argues that escalating sanctions without diplomatic off-ramps risks pushing Iran toward a North Korea-style nuclear breakout, noting how Pyongyang accelerated weapons development after the collapsed Hanoi summit in 2019.
Business · Korea Economic Daily
NPS to ease single-fund rule for Korean VC managers it backs
South Korea's National Pension Service plans to relax its single-fund restriction for venture capital managers it supports, allowing them to manage multiple funds simultaneously. The policy shift aims to enhance Korea's startup ecosystem competitiveness and follows criticism that the current rule hampers VC firms' growth compared to global peers who typically manage multiple vintage funds.
