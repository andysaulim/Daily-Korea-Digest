SK Hynix overtook Samsung Electronics in market capitalization on Monday, becoming South Korea's most valuable listed company for the first time as AI-driven memory chip demand fueled a record KOSPI close above 9,100. Early export data released Monday showed a continued jump in Korea's chip exports, reflecting sustained global AI infrastructure buildout. US-Korea

Former Justice Minister Park Sung-jae sentenced to 25 years for insurrection role in Dec 2024 martial law

Seoul Central District Court sentenced former Justice Minister Park Sung-jae to 25 years in prison Monday, finding him guilty of playing a key role in the insurrection through his involvement in former President Yoon Suk Yeol's December 3, 2024 martial law declaration — exceeding the 20-year term special counsel Cho Eun-suk's team had sought. The court immediately placed Park under custody citing evidence destruction concerns and convicted him on both insurrection and abuse-of-power charges related to a meeting of senior ministry officials he called following the martial law declaration. The same special counsel team separately booked former Defense Minister Kim Yong-hyun on allegations that the Defense Intelligence Command was directed to provoke North Korea to manufacture a martial law pretext.

So what: The sentences and new bookings advance the special counsel's accountability process ahead of former President Yoon's own trial proceedings.

US-Korea

Realmeter: Lee Jae-myung disapproval (49.7%) exceeds approval (46.7%) for first time since June 2025 inauguration

President Lee Jae-myung's net approval turned negative for the first time since his inauguration in June 2025 per a Realmeter survey released Monday — approval fell 4.8 percentage points to 46.7% while disapproval rose 5.5 points to 49.7%. Realmeter attributed the decline to public backlash over ballot shortages during the June 3 local elections and intensifying ruling Democratic Party infighting. Outgoing Prime Minister Kim Min-seok simultaneously held a press conference implying current DPK leadership bears responsibility for the party's declining ratings before departing for China's Summer Davos Forum.

So what: The approval inversion directly affects the DPK's intra-party leadership race and Lee's political capital ahead of the PM succession and the upcoming assembly committee deadline.

Microsoft: North Korean hackers compromised 140+ packages in Mastra AI agent framework supply chain attack

Microsoft assessed with high confidence that North Korean cybercriminals conducted a sophisticated software supply chain attack compromising more than 140 software packages tied to Mastra, a popular framework for building AI agents and applications. The report, published Friday, marks one of the first confirmed DPRK supply chain attacks specifically targeting AI development infrastructure. The campaign reflects the DPRK cyber apparatus adapting its intrusion tradecraft to the expanding AI toolchain.

So what: The Mastra compromise places AI development pipelines — including those used by defense-adjacent technology firms — within the DPRK cyber threat perimeter.
