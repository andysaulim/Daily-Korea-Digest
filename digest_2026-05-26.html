DPRK

N. Korea fires CRBMs, artillery rockets toward Yellow Sea

North Korea fired multiple close-range ballistic missiles (CRBMs) and artillery rockets toward the Yellow Sea on Monday afternoon, according to South Korea's Joint Chiefs of Staff. The projectiles were launched from the Hwanghae Province area and flew westward into the sea. The launch marks the latest in a series of weapons tests by Pyongyang this year amid heightened tensions on the Korean Peninsula.

Pattern: Similar missile tests occurred on the same date in 2017 and 2009 according to CSIS provocation database records

S. Korea aims to launch 1st nuclear-powered submarine in mid-2030s

South Korea aims to develop domestically built nuclear-powered submarines and launch the first by the mid-2030s, the defense minister announced Tuesday. The minister emphasized that South Korea will comply with all nonproliferation obligations in the nuclear submarine project. President Lee Jae-myung separately instructed officials to accelerate efforts to acquire nuclear-powered submarine capabilities for enhanced naval deterrence.

So what: Directly affects upcoming Shangri-La Dialogue defense discussions and US-ROK nuclear cooperation framework negotiations
