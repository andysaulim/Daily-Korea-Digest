Top Stories

Appeals court increases ex-first lady sentence to 4 years for corruption

Seoul High Court increased former first lady Kim Keon Hee's sentence from 20 months to four years in prison on corruption charges. Court found her partially guilty of stock price manipulation scheme involving Deutsch Motors and guilty of accepting luxury gifts from Unification Church, while acquitting her of receiving free opinion poll results. Special counsel team had sought 15-year prison term for wife of former President Yoon Suk Yeol.

Yonhap English

Korea unveils plan to cut plastic use 30% by 2030

Ministry of Climate, Energy and Environment presented 'Plastic-Free Circular Economy Transition Plan' at Cabinet meeting targeting 30% reduction in newly produced plastic materials from projected levels by 2030. Plan addresses expected increase in plastic waste from 7.8 million tons in 2024 to 10 million tons by 2030 through source reduction and recycled material replacement.

Korea Times

Korean stock market tops $4.1 trillion, doubles in one year

Korean stock market capitalization exceeded $4.1 trillion, more than doubling from previous year levels as KOSPI reached record highs. The milestone reflects strong performance across Korean equities amid global market optimism and domestic economic recovery.
