Korea sees potential wartime operational control transfer as early as next year

Korea believes the final verification stage for retaking wartime operational control from the United States can be completed in about one year, potentially enabling transition as early as next year. The allies are currently at the second stage of verifying full operational capability and plan to complete FOC validation by end of this year. Korea seeks to regain wartime OPCON before President Lee Jae-myung's five-year term ends in 2030.

So what: On the agenda for annual Security Consultative Meeting to set target year
