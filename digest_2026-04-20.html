Top Stories

N. Korea tests cluster bombs in latest ballistic missile launch

North Korea tested cluster bomb warheads on ballistic missiles with Kim Jong Un and his daughter Ju Ae observing the launch. KCNA reported the test validated the dispersal and guidance performance of cluster munitions designed for tactical ballistic missiles. The test represents continued advancement in North Korea's conventional strike capabilities alongside its nuclear program.

Samsung SDI wins 1st EV battery supply deal from Mercedes-Benz

Samsung SDI secured its first electric vehicle battery supply contract with Mercedes-Benz for the German automaker's upcoming EV models. The deal marks Samsung SDI's entry into Mercedes-Benz's supply chain, competing directly with Chinese battery makers CATL and BYD. The contract value and timeline were not disclosed but represents a significant breakthrough for Korean battery technology in European premium vehicles.

POSCO, JSW Steel sign 10.7 tln-won deal to build steel plant in India

POSCO Group signed a 10.7 trillion won ($7.3 billion) agreement with India's JSW Steel to construct a integrated steel plant in India. The facility will have annual production capacity of 5 million tons and includes blast furnaces, steel mills, and downstream processing capabilities. The project represents POSCO's largest overseas investment and JSW Steel's expansion into premium steel grades for automotive and shipbuilding sectors.
