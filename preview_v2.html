ROK Politics · Yonhap
Early Voting Opens for Local Elections Across South Korea
Polling stations opened at 6 AM for two days of early voting ahead of Wednesday’s nationwide local elections. The National Election Commission reported 12.4% turnout by noon, tracking 2pp above the 2022 local election pace.
High early turnout historically favors the ruling party. If the Democratic Party sweeps both Seoul and Busan, Lee gains a mandate to push his economic reform package.
US-Korea · Reuters
Lee, Trump Agree to Bilateral Meeting at Shangri-La Dialogue
The two leaders spoke by phone for 40 minutes on Monday evening, agreeing to meet on the sidelines in Singapore on Saturday. The agenda includes the Section 122 surcharge extension, Samsung’s CHIPS Act commitment, and coordination on the stalled US-DPRK track.
