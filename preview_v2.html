Early Voting Opens for Local Elections Across South Korea
Polling stations opened at 6 AM for two days of early voting ahead of Wednesday’s nationwide local elections. Samsung Foundry Wins Qualcomm Snapdragon 9 Gen 4 Contract
Samsung’s semiconductor division will manufacture Qualcomm’s next-generation flagship mobile processor on its 2nm GAA process, ending TSMC’s three-year exclusive hold on premium Snapdragon chips. The deal is valued at an estimated $1.8B over two years and validates Samsung’s yield recovery at the Pyeongtaek P4 fab.
