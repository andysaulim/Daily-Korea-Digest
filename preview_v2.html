Early Voting Opens for Local Elections Across South Korea
Polling stations opened at 6 AM for two days of early voting ahead of Wednesday’s nationwide local elections. The National Election Commission reported 12.4% turnout by noon, tracking 2pp above the 2022 local election pace. Seoul and Busan gubernatorial races are seen as the key tests of Lee’s first-year governing mandate.
High early turnout historically favors the ruling party. If DP sweeps both cities, Lee gains a mandate to push his economic reform package through the National Assembly.

Lee, Trump Agree to Bilateral Meeting at Shangri-La Dialogue
The two leaders spoke by phone for 40 minutes on Monday evening, agreeing to meet on the sidelines of the Shangri-La Dialogue in Singapore on Saturday.
