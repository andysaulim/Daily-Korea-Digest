40,000 fans gather for BTS comeback concert in downtown Seoul

Heavy police presence secured downtown Seoul with road closures and security checkpoints. Concert featured new album 'Arirang' with themes from Korean history and traditional folk music.

So what: Demonstrates soft power projection and cultural diplomacy importance in Korean foreign policy toolkit.

Factory fire at car parts manufacturing facility killed 14 workers and injured 60 others in industrial accident.
