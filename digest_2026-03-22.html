Officials say 14 were killed in fire at South Korean auto parts plant

Fire at Daejeon auto parts factory killed 14 workers and injured 59 others in one of South Korea's deadliest workplace accidents this year. President Lee Jae Myung visited the site and ordered thorough investigation into safety violations. Incident highlights ongoing industrial safety concerns in Korean manufacturing sector.

So what: Likely to prompt renewed government focus on workplace safety regulations and enforcement
