President Lee's approval rating dips to 59.3%

President Lee Jae-myung's approval rating fell slightly to 59.3 percent in the latest Realmeter poll conducted May 19-23, down from previous highs above 60 percent. The Democratic Party maintained 44.1 percent support while the opposition People Power Party held 23.8 percent ahead of June 3 local elections. The slight decline comes as manufacturing confidence rebounds but concerns persist over high interest rates and exchange rates.

All parties place AI at center of local election pledges

Political parties across Korea's spectrum are putting artificial intelligence at the heart of campaign promises for June 3 local elections, with every parliamentary party mentioning AI in its top 10 pledges. The Democratic Party lists AI vision as one of three top priorities, while proposals range from attracting a UN AI center to building AI-based administrative systems and expanding AI-enabled safety nets for seniors. The consensus signals AI will shape everything from growth policy to welfare and public administration.
