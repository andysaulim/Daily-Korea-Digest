Kim Jong Un meets Russian Duma speaker in Pyongyang ahead of Kursk memorial

North Korean leader Kim Jong Un met State Duma Chairman Vyacheslav Volodin in Pyongyang as Russia prepares to inaugurate a memorial honoring DPRK troops killed fighting in Ukraine's Kursk region. Volodin thanked North Korea for its assistance in the fight against what he called 'neo-Nazi invaders' and called for expanded cooperation between Russian and North Korean legislators. The visit underscores deepening military ties as both nations face international isolation.

So what: Directly relevant to ongoing NK-Russia Axis research by CSIS Korea Chair

Pattern: Russia officially confirmed North Korean troop deployment in Ukraine on Apr 26, 2025

Russian delegation visits N. Korea to open museum for soldiers killed in Ukraine

A Russian delegation led by Duma speaker Vyacheslav Volodin arrived in North Korea to inaugurate a museum honoring DPRK soldiers killed in the Ukraine war. Russian Interior Minister Vladimir Kolokoltsev praised North Koreans as having 'proved themselves Russia's true friends' while commemorating Soviet soldiers who liberated Korea. The memorial's opening marks unprecedented public acknowledgment of North Korean combat casualties in the conflict.

So what: First formal memorial to DPRK troops killed in Ukraine signals permanence of military cooperation

Pattern: Russia committed to military aid for North Korea under bilateral treaty on Apr 28, 2025

Yonhap English

OECD projects S. Korea's potential growth to hit record low 1.52% in late 2027

South Korea's potential growth rate is projected to fall to a record low of 1.52 percent in late 2027, according to new OECD forecasts that underscore the economy's structural challenges. The projection reflects declining labor force participation due to rapid aging and stagnant productivity growth across key sectors. The forecast adds pressure on policymakers to implement structural reforms as the Lee administration faces its first electoral test in June local elections.

So what: Affects BOK rate decisions at May 22 and Jul 10 monetary policy meetings
