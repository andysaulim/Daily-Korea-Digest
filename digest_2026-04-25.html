Top Stories

Russia's Duma speaker arrives in Pyongyang for Ukraine war memorial

Vyacheslav Volodin, speaker of Russia's State Duma, landed in North Korea Friday for working visit centered on opening museum honoring Russian soldiers killed in Ukraine conflict. Visit marks deepening military-political cooperation as Pyongyang continues supplying artillery shells and missiles to Russian forces while deploying construction workers and potentially combat troops to support Moscow's war effort.

So what: Reinforces NK-Russia axis ahead of Apr 26 anniversary of 2019 Kim-Putin summit

Pattern: Russia confirmed North Korean troop deployment in Ukraine one year ago (Apr 26, 2025)

Yonhap / TASS

Prosecutors demand 30-year prison term for Yoon over Pyongyang drone flights

South Korean prosecutors sought maximum 30-year sentence for ousted President Yoon Suk Yeol Friday on charges he ordered military drones to fly over Pyongyang in October 2025. Alleged flights carrying propaganda leaflets sparked North Korean threats to destroy drone launch sites and contributed to domestic crisis culminating in Yoon's December 2025 impeachment and January 2026 removal from office.

So what: Sets precedent for prosecuting former presidents on national security charges

Washington Post

Seoul-Washington seek intelligence 'normalization' after surveillance scandal

Senior South Korean diplomat confirmed Friday that Seoul and Washington are working to normalize relations following U.S. restrictions on intelligence-sharing with ROK agencies. Curbs reportedly imposed after discovery of unauthorized South Korean surveillance operations targeting American officials and facilities in Korea, creating most serious alliance trust crisis since 2013 NSA revelations about U.S. eavesdropping on Korean leaders.

So what: Could affect real-time intelligence sharing on North Korean missile launches
