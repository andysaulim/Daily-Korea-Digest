US-ROK launch largest-ever combined logistics exercise — 4,400 troops, 600 equipment pieces

The Korea-US Combined Forces Command launched the largest-ever combined joint sustainment training (CJST) on July 14, involving 4,400 Korean and US troops and approximately 600 pieces of equipment including warships and aircraft, running four days near Pohang in North Gyeongsang Province and Hongcheon in Gangwon Province. The exercise includes the Dragon Lift component, involving medical helicopter transport of injured personnel. The record scale comes 12 days before the July 27 armistice anniversary, a period DPRK has historically used for escalatory signaling.

So what — Serves as a readiness demonstration ahead of the July 27 armistice anniversary and the anticipated Ulchi Freedom Shield exercise in August.

NK Pro: North Korea bulldozes drone base, grandstands near complete ahead of military demonstration

NK Pro analysis of Maxar and Planet Labs satellite imagery shows North Korea has bulldozed old structures at its main drone base in recent weeks and is close to completing large grandstands assessed as preparation for a military demonstration for hundreds of officials. The activity follows Kim Jong Un's June 25 plenum directive and his July 11–12 formal state decision to expand DPRK nuclear forces and military intelligence operations. The demonstration would likely feature DPRK's expanding drone arsenal, which Kim has publicly prioritized for use against South Korea.

So what — Drone demonstration event expected in the lead-up to the July 27 armistice anniversary, a date DPRK uses for military showcases.
