Top Stories

South Korea's exports top $80 billion for second consecutive month in April

South Korea's exports exceeded $80 billion for the second straight month in April, rising 48% year-on-year to reach $80.4 billion driven by strong semiconductor demand. Chip exports surged 89.1% while petroleum products and ships also posted significant gains, offsetting declines in automobiles and textiles.

So what: Reinforces Korea's position as a key AI supply chain hub ahead of the May 22 BOK rate decision.

Ministry of Trade, Industry and Energy data via Yonhap

Kim Jong Un praises soldier suicide attacks in Ukraine

Kim Jong Un lauded North Korean troops for choosing 'self-detonation' rather than capture by Ukrainian forces, according to CNN reporting. The comments represent the first official DPRK acknowledgment of North Korean military casualties in Ukraine and Kim's direct praise for troops killed in combat.

So what: Confirms DPRK troop deployments to Ukraine and signals potential escalation in NK-Russia military cooperation.

CNN citing North Korean state media

South Korean envoy stresses commitment to 'peaceful coexistence' on peninsula

Ambassador Cha Ji-hoon delivered a speech at the UN Security Council reaffirming Seoul's commitment to peaceful coexistence on the Korean Peninsula and denuclearization. The address came as the US and other countries marked two years since Russia vetoed a resolution to extend the UN expert panel monitoring North Korea sanctions enforcement.

So what: Positions Seoul's diplomatic approach ahead of potential US-DPRK engagement following the May 14-15 Trump-Xi summit.
